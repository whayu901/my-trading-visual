✅ Pre-Entry Checklist

Before Every Trade - Check These!

✅ GREEN LIGHT (Trade!)

  • Diamond (◆) muncul
  • Volume >1000 (high)
  • Candlestick pattern jelas
  • M15 zone align dengan M5
  • Minimal 3 konfirmasi
  • RR minimum 1:2
  • Fresh zone (1-2 touch)

❌ RED LIGHT (Skip!)

  • No diamond signal
  • Volume <500 (low)
  • Zone touched 4+ times
  • Against HTF trend
  • Major news in 30 min
  • RR less than 1:1.5
  • Asian session (XAU)

🎯 S/R + Trend Combo Strategy

Kombinasi Support/Resistance dengan Trend Detection untuk Trading Akurat

📊 Support & Resistance (SNR)

Fungsi: Mendeteksi zona entry (WHERE to enter)

  • 🟢 Zona Hijau = Support (area bounce)
  • 🔴 Zona Merah = Resistance (area reject)
  • 🟠 Zona Orange = SBR/RBS (flip zone)
  • 💎 Diamond ◆ = Entry signal
Kekuatan: Presisi entry point tinggi
Kelemahan: Tidak tahu trend direction

📈 Trend Detector

Fungsi: Mendeteksi arah trend (WHICH direction to trade)

  • 📈 EMA 9/21/50 = Trend filter
  • 📊 RSI = Momentum confirmation
  • 🟢 BUY Label = Uptrend detected
  • 🔴 SELL Label = Downtrend detected
Kekuatan: Tahu arah trend
Kelemahan: Entry sering late, no exact zone
⚡ KOMBINASI KEDUANYA = PERFECT SETUP! ⚡

🎯 Strategi Combo S/R + Trend

Konsep: Gunakan Trend Detector untuk tahu arah, lalu cari entry di zona S/R

✅ GOLDEN RULE:
  • 🔼 UPTREND → Hanya ambil BUY signal dari S/R (zona support/RBS)
  • 🔽 DOWNTREND → Hanya ambil SELL signal dari S/R (zona resistance/SBR)
  • ↔️ SIDEWAYS → Trade both directions (hati-hati!)

⚙️ Recommended Settings

  • Lookback Period: 10 (default)
  • Volume Filter: 2 (scalping)

  • Fast EMA: 9 (default)
  • Slow EMA: 21 (default)
  • Trend EMA: 50 (filter)

📝 Step-by-Step Trading Process

Sebelum Market Open:

  1. Load 2 indicators: Support & Resistance + Trend Detector
  2. Check higher timeframe trend (H1/H4) untuk bias harian
  3. Mark important S/R zones di H1

Saat Trading (M5/M15):

  1. Check Trend Detector → Lihat table "Trading Bias"
  2. Wait for Price → Tunggu harga mendekati zona S/R
  3. Look for Diamond Signal → Harus sesuai dengan trend!
  4. Confirm ALL checklist (trend + S/R + diamond)
  5. ENTRY → Set SL & TP
  6. Monitor → Breakeven setelah profit 1:1
  7. Close → Hit TP atau trailing stop
